ECO is our DNA

Climate change and pollution have changed the thinking of our product development. Starting from raw materials, we try to reduce the use of chemical fibers as much as possible instate of using more environmentally friendly raw materials and processes to reduce environmental pollution.

HFI is striving to be an environmental steward partnering with both Bluesign and GRS 4.0.
At HFI, we are proud to offer so many Eco-friendly textile technologies in both knit textile to satisfy our partner’s apparel needs.

Our action plan includes

1. Environmentally friendly polyester: recycled polyester fiber/biomass/decomposable polyester

2. Reduce microplastic pollution: cellulose fiber/natural fiber blend

3. Innovative 3D fabric design to adapt to climate change
